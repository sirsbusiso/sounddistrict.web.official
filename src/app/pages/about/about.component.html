About Sound District

More Than Music.
A Home for House Music Culture.

Sound District is a South African music platform dedicated to showcasing the finest Deep House, Soulful House, Afro House and underground electronic music. We believe great music deserves a home where artists and listeners can connect through authentic sounds.

From exclusive podcast episodes and guest mixes to merchandise and artist collaborations, Sound District exists to celebrate creativity, discover new talent and build a community that lives for quality music.
